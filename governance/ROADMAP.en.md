## How to Contribute to the Roadmap

The roadmap is a living document that evolves based on:

1. **Community feedback**: Discussions and feature requests
2. **Research findings**: Evidence from user studies
3. **Industry needs**: Real-world implementation challenges
4. **Technological changes**: New platforms and interaction methods

To propose additions or changes:

1. Open a GitHub Discussion in the "Roadmap" category
2. Describe the proposed addition or change
3. Provide rationale and expected impact
4. Engage with community feedback
5. Core Team will evaluate and update roadmap

## Dependencies and Risks

### Dependencies
- Community contributor engagement
- Access to senior users for testing
- Partnership development
- Resource availability

### Risks
- Slow adoption by industry
- Competing frameworks or standards
- Resource constraints
- Rapidly changing technology landscape

### Mitigation Strategies
- Focus on evidence-based approach
- Build strong partnerships
- Maintain flexibility in specifications
- Engage early with standards bodies
- Prioritize practical, implementable guidance

## Current Priorities

**Top 3 priorities for Q1 2026:**

1. **Complete Core Specifications**: Finish trilingual specifications for all four pillars with testable requirements
2. **Validation with Users**: Test specifications with actual senior users and incorporate feedback
3. **Community Building**: Establish active contributor community and communication channels
